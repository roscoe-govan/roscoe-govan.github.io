Capstone Excellence Award

Western Governors University • M.S. in IT Management

Overview

This page highlights my Capstone Excellence Award from Western Governors University and provides a summary of the capstone project that supported that recognition.

The capstone is a 56-page post-implementation report centered on a Starlink network failover initiative designed to improve resiliency, reduce business impact from outages, and strengthen long-term operational continuity.

Read Online Download PDF

Project Summary

In the report, I present a project scenario in which severe business loss was tied to a lack of network redundancy across store locations. The proposed solution was to use Starlink as a failover technology to improve resilience, support continuity, and reduce the financial impact of outages.

The project documentation includes the business case, project planning artifacts, risk analysis, milestone tracking, validation methods, implementation results, stakeholder communication, and ongoing support planning.

Key Highlights

Business Problem

The report frames the business problem around limited redundancy, approximately 83% annual availability, and an estimated $4 million in annual loss tied to outages.

Project Scope

The capstone covers planning, risk management, communication, implementation, testing, documentation, and post-implementation support for a 500-store rollout.

Execution & Milestones

The rollout is tracked through milestone-based execution, including team readiness, equipment arrival, 200 installations, 400 installations, 500 installations, and deliverable acceptance.

Outcome Orientation

The report emphasizes measurable outcomes, evidence-based validation, and project completion ahead of the originally proposed close date.

What This Work Demonstrates

Structured Project Leadership

The report shows disciplined planning across scope, milestones, resource requirements, testing, documentation, and closure.

Business-Minded Technical Thinking

The capstone ties technology decisions to availability, cost, business continuity, and long-term operational value.

Execution with Measurable Outcomes

The work focuses not just on proposing a technical solution, but on validating outcomes, documenting acceptance criteria, and thinking through sustainment after implementation.
