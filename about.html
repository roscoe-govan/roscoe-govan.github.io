About

Professional background, technical experience, and career direction.

Professional Summary

I am a technical professional with experience across cybersecurity investigations, enterprise network administration, incident analysis, identity-related investigations, root cause analysis, and technical documentation. My background includes supporting large-scale enterprise environments where accuracy, communication, and defensible decision-making are critical.

My work is grounded in structured problem solving, evidence-based analysis, and clear communication. Whether investigating security activity, troubleshooting network issues, or documenting findings for stakeholders, I focus on producing work that is accurate, useful, and aligned with long-term business value.

Enterprise IT & Cybersecurity Experience

My career began in hands-on infrastructure and technical support roles, where I developed a strong foundation in troubleshooting, connectivity analysis, customer support, device support, and service restoration. I later moved into enterprise network administration, supporting complex network and connectivity issues across large-scale environments.

I then transitioned into cybersecurity, applying that same investigative discipline to security incidents, identity activity, endpoint telemetry, SIEM data, threat intelligence enrichment, and incident documentation. This combination of networking, infrastructure, and cybersecurity experience gives me a practical understanding of both technical systems and the business impact of secure, reliable operations.

Current Career Focus

I am currently focused on mid-level to senior-level IT roles that are stable, quality-focused, and aligned with my technical background. I am also interested in quality-focused cybersecurity roles where analysis, accuracy, documentation, risk reduction, and durable outcomes matter more than high-volume task throughput.

My strongest areas of interest include infrastructure, IAM, vulnerability management, GRC/risk, technical program or project work, network security, cybersecurity analysis, and leadership-track opportunities. I am especially interested in roles where technical depth, thoughtful communication, and long-term value are emphasized.

Credentials & Leadership Development

I hold a Master of Science in IT Management from Western Governors University and a Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity from Hallmark University, where I graduated as class valedictorian. My technical certifications include GCIH, CCNA, CompTIA Security+, and CompTIA Network+.

In addition to technical development, I have completed leadership-focused programs including the Dale Carnegie Breakthrough to Success course and NSLS Advanced Leadership Certification. These experiences reflect my interest in combining technical execution with communication, professional maturity, and leadership potential.

Work Philosophy

I value quality over rushed output. My strongest work happens in environments where accuracy, documentation, root cause analysis, and thoughtful decision-making are expected. I believe technical work should not only solve the immediate issue, but also leave behind a clearer understanding of what happened, why it happened, and how similar issues can be prevented or handled more effectively in the future.

I approach technical challenges with discipline, curiosity, and accountability. I aim to communicate clearly, document thoroughly, validate assumptions, and support decisions with evidence. My goal is to contribute to teams that value reliable outcomes, professional judgment, and continuous improvement.

Purpose of This Portfolio

This website serves as a professional portfolio for my resume, technical projects, academic work, cybersecurity learning, and long-term career development. It is designed to give hiring managers, recruiters, and technical peers a clearer view of my background, interests, and approach to solving problems.

Visitors can use this site to learn more about my experience, review my resume, explore selected work such as my capstone project, and connect with me through LinkedIn or the contact page.
